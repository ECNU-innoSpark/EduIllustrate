Ultraman travels from ($30^\circ N, 120^\circ E$) through the center of the Earth. The exit point is the antipodal point. * Latitude: Changes from North to South (same magnitude). $30^\circ N \rightarrow 30^\circ S$. * Longitude: Changes by $180^\circ$ (supplementary angle to 180, opposite East/West). * $180^\circ - 120^\circ = 60^\circ$. * East becomes West. * Result: $60^\circ W$.

Longitude Comparison: E is at $20^\circ E$ and F is at $40^\circ E$. Since $20^\circ$ is smaller than $40^\circ$ (and both are East), E is to the left of F. Therefore, E is West of F.

Conclusion: The line separating the Temperate Zone (North) and the Tropical Zone (South) in the Northern Hemisphere is the Tropic of Cancer ($23.5^\circ N$).
